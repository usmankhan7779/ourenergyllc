Green Electricity:  Benefits for Today and Tomorrow

What Is it Green Electricity?

Green Electricity, Green Energy or Renewable Energy are the alternative energy sources, occurring naturally that are renewable, sustainable and clean.  The main types of green energy are:solar, wind, hydroelectric, Geothermal and Biomass.  For more information, view About Green Energy.

What’s the Big Deal?

Much better than green eggs and ham!  Green Electricity is all over the media these days.  Sure, you would agree that those huge wind turbines look lovely on our postcards, but what is the big deal? What does green energy mean to me as a Texan?

The Bottom Line:  Green Electricity is Beneficial to Texas Today and Tomorrow.

5 CHANGES that WE WANT IN TEXAS!

The following are five changes that we will all be happy about.  Everything is BIG in Texas, but we will all agree that these are five things that we would love to see reduced.

  • 1.Pollution.

  • Reduces Air and Water Pollution.Green energy means clean energy options allow us to enjoy a cleaner land!

  • 2.Health Impacts.

  • Reduces Health Impacts. Cleaner air and water means less breathing problems, heart attacks and cancer.

  • 3. Foreign Dependence.

  • Reduces Dependence on Imported Fuels.Renewable energy creates more jobs here in Texas!

  • 4.  Crazy Weather. 

  • Reduces the Impacts on the Environment.  Clean energy slows down Global Warming.  Which is driving that increasingly “crazy weather” and natural disasters all over the world.

  • 5.Price Swings.

  • It only goes down.  No more price swings!  As technologies improve green electricity has steadily dipped in price becoming accessible to all.
  • Source:  Union of Concerned Scientists

Green Electricity looks beautiful on our Texan Landscapes, in our homes, our businesses and in our future!
