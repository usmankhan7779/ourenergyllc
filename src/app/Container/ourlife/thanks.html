Thanksgiving Cranberry Chutney RECIPE

Serves 20 Total Prep Time:Approximately 10 Minutes Cooking Time: Approximately 5 Minutes

Cranberry Chutney

Ingredients:

  • 2 cans of whole cranberries
  • 1 can de cranberry jelly
  • 1 cup of golden raisins
  • 5 apples chopped into small bite sized cubes
  • 3 pears chopped into small bite sized cubes
  • 4 teaspoons of red vinegar
  • 5 cups of brown sugar
  • 1 small red onion finely minced.
  • 1 medium white onion finely minced.
  • 10 whole cloves
  • 4 whole cinnamon sticks
  • 4 tablespoons of grated fresh ginger (or 1 tablespoon of powdered ginger)
  • 5 thin slices of lemon (cut a lemon in half and slide the sides so that you obtain thin round slices)
  • A dash of salt
  • 1/3 cup of crumbled pecans

Instructions:

  •  P lace all the ingredients into a large pot, mix well until boiling. Cover and cook for 5 minutes on medium temperature. Take off of the stove to cool.  The chutney is served at room temperature.

Serving Variations:

Tip:  For a juicier mixture , you may add ¼ cup of apple or orange juice to the recipe.

A la Mode Dessert Alternative: Pour the Cranberry Chutney on top of vanilla ice cream!

Fresh Alternative:  Substitute the canned cranberries for fresh ones whenever you can.  You may have to adjust the sugar to keep it from becoming too tart.

Another Alternative: Try experimenting with other types of berries and fruits like red pears to give its flavor a whole new dimension!
