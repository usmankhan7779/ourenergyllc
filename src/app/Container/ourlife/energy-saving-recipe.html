TEXAS BBQ Beer Basted Hot Dogs in Pineapple Chipotle Sauce

RECIPE

Serves 6

Beer Basted Hot Dogs

Ingredients:

  • – 6 Hot Dogs (of your favorite kind)
  • – 6 Hot Dog Buns
  • –  1 Onion Chopped into Rings
  • – 4 Minced Cloves of Garlic
  • – 1 Can of Beer
  • – 2 Tablespoons of Tomato Paste
  • – 1 Tablespoon of Olive Oil
  • – 1 Laurel Leaf
  • –  Dash of Sal and Black Pepper to Taste
  • – Chili (Optional)

Instructions:

  • – Add the onions, garlic, tomato paste, olive oil, beer and laurel leaf to a small disposable aluminum roasting pan.
  • –  Mix and season with salt, pepper and chili to taste.
  • –  Add the hot dogs and place the roasting pan on the grill, simmering well but not allowing the liquid to fully dry out.
  • –  Add the hot dogs to the grill until well browned.  By basting the hot dogs first, they will be crispy on the outside and tender on the inside.
  • –  Add the hot dogs to the buns.

Pineapple Chipotle Sauce Topping

Ingredients:

  • – 1 Small Pineapple Peeled and Diced or 1 Large Can of Pineapple Chunks
  • – 1 Red Bell Pepper Diced
  • – 1 Small Onion Diced
  • –  3 Tablespoons of Fresh Cilantro Chopped
  • – 3 Tablespoons of Lime Juice
  • – 3 Tablespoons of Canned Chipotle Chile in Adobo (Chiles and the Sauce) Chopped up

Instructions:

  • – Mix up all of the ingredients in a bowl and you’re ready to go!
  • – You may consider making a bigger batch to save it for later. The dressing covered and chilled may last up to three days in the refrigerator.
  • – Once the dressing is done, dollop it over the Beer Basted Hot Dogs and let us know how it goes!
  • – Serve with Tortilla chips.

Serving Variations:

Day Before Alternative: You may leave the hot dogs basting overnight and prepare the pineapple chipotle sauce the day before.  This way you have everything ready to just grill and go the next day.

Spicy Alternative: For those of us who love LIFE SPICY, you may add a bit of jalapeno or Serrano pepper into the blend.

Bacon Lover’s Alternative: Wrap the hot dogs in bacon before cooking.  It’s sinfully delicious!
