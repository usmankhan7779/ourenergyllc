LED LIGHTS: Bringing Science Fiction Alive!

By now, I’m sure that you have heard about how LED lights are far superior to the traditional incandescent light bulbs and the alternative compact fluorescents (CFLs). But, how much do you really know about how their being used today?

Light Emitting Diode is not the cousin of the lightsaber, but could be. The actual name of LED not only sounds like something out of a sci-fi flick, it’s brought science fiction into today’s world.

COLOR ME RED

What do traffic lights, televisions and your brake lights all have in common? You guessed it, LED lights. LED’s are powered by a cool phenomenon called electroluminescence .   Although this was first discovered back in 1907, they didn’t actually create the first LED until 1962 when it barely emitted a pale red light. It became commercial in the nineties and more affordable in the 2000’s.  In recent years, the most exciting uses have began to pop up.  We’ve come a long way!

AMAZING USES OF LED

Originally, LED was very expensive but with continued improvements over the years, it has become very affordable and has opened the door to creative uses.  With continued improvements in quality and decrease in price over the years, LED has been used in ways that its creator would have never dreamed of. Here are five of the most unusual ways people have used LEDs.

TOP FIVE Creative Uses of LED

5.  Move Over Rembrandt.

From sculptures to large displays, LED ART has brought about a new era in street art and an exciting new medium for artists.

4.  Bed Time Magic.

You will never be afraid of the dark again! Sleep surrounded by the warm glow of a plush LED pillow and sheet. These lights are teeny tiny in size do not give off too much heat, making them a delight to fall asleep in.

3.  Light up the Party!

Can’t dance? Tongue-tied? No problem! Want to be the life of a party without making a sound? Try on an LED dress, shirt or hat and you’ll be sure to stand out in the crowd. If wearing an entire dress may sound a bit too loud for your style, try on a pair of LED earrings or lace up some LED shoelaces for a more subtle glow.

2.  Artificial Northern Lights.

The Waterlicht, a creation of artist Daan Roosegaarde simulates the beauty of the reflection of light on water created by LEDs. Watch the inspiring video, it will take your breath away!

The Waterlicht

1.  Texas Style.

The number one of the top five creative uses of LED goes to horse tail lights! Get your horses in on the FUN! If you think you look great in LED, you should see how great it looks on your horse. Your loyal friend will not only look beautiful (more than usual) but also be much safer at night.   Inventor, Sammi Gross insures that using these fancy “tail lights” from Kickstarter will avoid accidents as cars can see your horse up to a mile away.  As they say, “Be Visible.  Save Your Tail.”  Don’t you just love it!
