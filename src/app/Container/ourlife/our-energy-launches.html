OUR Energy Launches New Website
  • Our Energy Llc
  • Energy News Our Energy Buzz
  • No comments

Welcome to the new face of OUR Energy. We are delighted to announce our brand new OUR Energy website with an enhanced online experience! At OUR Energy, we treasure you as a customer and are always trying to find new ways of going the extra mile. You deserve it!

Bring on the Good News!

Let’s face it. Some days it seems like nothing works out right. It’s one of those, “I just got out of the wrong side of the bed” days. If you are experiencing this type of day, it’s about to change! Your premier Texas energy provider just upgraded your service! With OUR Energy’s new website comes a windfall of New OUR Energy Member perks. Here’s a sneak peak at some of them:

  • OUR Life Blog.

This is our fun new way to make sure that you are kept up to date and informed on energy news and much more. We added a zap of energy to the usual energy news (boring!). At the OUR Life Blog we’ll provide you with insightful ways to Save Money, keep up to date on Texas Events, Win PRIZES (yippee!) and much, much more. We’d like to give all of you a warm Texas welcome to OUR Life!

  • Energy Saving Tips.

Find practical ways to save energy. Let’s be honest, we can all save money these days. At OUR Energy, we’ve whipped up some ways to help you save money on your electric bill that are simple to apply. Make sure to keep up to date on our Energy Tips to get the most bang for your buck.

  • Help Tools.

We’re always finding ways to make things easier for you. In the Help Tools area, our goal is to provide you with a wealth of valuable information. The next time you have a question, we invite you to check out our FAQs in our Help Tools to see if you answer is posted there. What if the question and answer is not posted? Our new Help Tools will continuously grow with the needs and feedback from our customers. We invite you to submit your questions and suggestions today!

  • OUR Community

This is a brand new section that we are particularly proud of.   We have recently launched an OUR Energy Community Outreach program:  OUR Community. Here, you can find out about wonderful opportunities to help improve our community, keep up to date on the projects that OUR Energy is sponsoring and even submit a suggested non-profit project. Visit the Community page for more information. Together we can make a difference!

  • Member Specials

This is the stuff that dreams are made of! At OUR Energy, we don’t just say “Thank you”, we’re making it fun, exciting and rewarding for you. We’re running a great variety of promotions that everyone will enjoy: on the spot specials, weekly, monthly and more!  Don’t miss out on being our next winner.  Visit the Specials page to get in on the fun!

It pays to be an OUR Energy customer. If you’re not one, make sure that you Sign Up today!

  • Let’s Keep ‘Em Coming!

We need to make sure that you’ll always be the main focus of OUR Energy! We’d love to hear from you to keep our service fresh and awesome for all. Please feel free to contact webmaster@ourenergyllc.com with any suggestions or feedback that you may have.

Thanks Y’all for being a part of the OUR Energy Family!
