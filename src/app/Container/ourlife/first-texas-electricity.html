FIRST TEXAS ELECTRICITY OUTREACH PROJECT: NEW HOPE HOUSING

OUR Energy is proud to announce their very first community outreach project:  New Hope Housing!

Thank you New Hope Housing for giving us the opportunity to give back to the beautiful Texas community! ‪#‎ powertochangelives‬

If you know of a project that you would like for us to sponsor, please feel free to visit our Texas Electricity Community Outreach pages.

In the summer of 2015, New Hope Housing formed a great partnership with Our Energy, one of Texas’ fastest growing privately owned electricity providers. Demonstrating their strong commitment to serving the needs of our community, Our Energy has recently launched their Texas Electricity Community Outreach Program. The program combines the power of cost savings with community development tools to make a positive impact. Our Energy will soon provide power to all New Hope Housing properties.

ABOUT NEW HOPE HOUSING

For more than 20 years, New Hope Housing has transformed lives through affordable, supportive housing. Our high-quality apartment communities are specially designed to restore dignity and inspire success in people living on extremely low incomes. Through New Hope Housing’s Resident Service Program, residents receive ongoing support that is essential for achieving long-term stability and breaking the cycle of poverty and homelessness. By working together to create change, New Hope Housing and Our Energy are committed to creating affordable solutions that have long lasting impacts. – Source New Hope Housing.
