Texas Caviar with Zesty Avocado Cilantro Dressing RECIPE

Serves 10
Total Prep and Serve Time:  Approximately 40 Mins.

Texas Caviar

Prep Time:  35 Mins.

Ingredients:

  •  –  1 (15 ounce) can of black beans, rinsed and drained
  •  –  1 (15 ounce) can of black-eyed peas, rinsed and drained
  •  –  2 (15 ounce) cans of pinto beans, rinsed and drained
  •  –  2 (15 ounce) cans of corn, rinsed and drained
  •  –  1 red bell pepper, seeded and chopped
  •  –  1 green bell pepper, seeded and chopped
  •  –  1 yellow bell pepper, seeded and chopped
  •  –  1 small red onion, finely chopped
  •  –  1 pint of cherry tomatoes, quartered
  •  –  1 – 3 jalapeno peppers, seeded, and chopped (optional)

Instructions:

  • – Mix the black beans, pinto beans, black-eyed peas, corn, bell peppers, jalapeno pepper, red onion and tomatoes together in a large bowl.

Zesty Avocado Cilantro Dressing

Prep Time: 5 Mins.

Ingredients:

  • –  1 avocado
  • –  ¼ cup Greek Yogurt or Sour Cream
  • –  1 cup chopped Cilantro leaves and stems
  • –  2 cloves of Garlic
  • –  ½ teaspoon Salt or to taste
  • –  1 tablespoon of fresh Lime or Lemon juice (or White Vinegar)
  • –  2 tablespoons of Olive Oil
  • –  Ground black Pepper to Taste(optional)
  • –  Approximately ½ Cup of Water

Instructions:

  • –  Toss all of the ingredients in a food processor or blender and pulse away, scraping the sides until smooth.
  • –  You may consider making a bigger batch to save it for later.  The dressing covered and chilled may last up to three days in the refrigerator.
  • –  Once the dressing is done, add it to the Texas Caviar mixture (little by little) and mix until well blended.
  • –  Serve with Tortilla chips.

Serving Variations:

Creamy Alternative: You may substitute buttermilk for the water for a creamier dressing.

Spicy Alternative: For those of us who love LIFE SPICY, you may add a bit of jalapeno or Serrano pepper into the blend.

Another Alternative: If the sauce turns out too sour for your taste, here’s a great tip! Just add a bit of honey (very little at a time) until it’s just right!
