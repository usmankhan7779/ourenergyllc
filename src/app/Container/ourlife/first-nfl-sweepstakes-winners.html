Congratulations to Our Energy’s
First NFL Sweepstakes Winners!

After much anticipation, we are pleased to announce the winner of our first ever NFL Sweepstakes from the Live the Touchdowns Specials.

We are honored to award this prize to:
Melissa Cruz from Tromball, Texas!

Melissa won NFL Tickets for two after entering our first ever NFL Sweepstakes contest.  The first contest of many as we launched our New Website and OUR Life Blog.  On Monday morning, OUR Energy VP, Andrew Bejarano called Melissa to deliver the good news.  The following is an excerpt of the conversation:

Andrew: Hello, is this Melissa Cruz?
Melissa :  Yes, it is.

Andrew:  Did you enter an NFL Sweepstakes contest on our website?
Melissa :  Oh, yes.  I did.

Andrew:  My name is Andrew, I’m a VP of OUR Energy calling to give you some good news… Congratulations!  You won the tickets!
Melissa :  Awesome!  OK!

Andrew: I noticed that you are a new customer.  How did you first hear about us and why did you decide to switch over?
Melissa :  I found you guys online and saw that you have good rates. In San Antonio we only have TXU energy. We didn’t have a choice. When we first moved here we were with Champion Energy. But after a year’s contract my bill went up.  I saw that you guys had a good prices and I decided to switch.

Andrew:  Well, I have some good news for you.  Our rates are about to get even better.  We are going to start making plans available giving away free nights and free weekends.
Melissa :  Really?  Well, TXU have been soliciting door to door but I did the math and I knew that wouldn’t work out overall.

Andrew: Well, we have a special advantage to be able to deliver you the free nights and free weekends and keep our rates low.  Essentially, when we save money – you save money based on our purchasing practices.
Melissa :  Well, I’m happy so far.

Andrew:  Do you have any recommendations for us?
Melissa :  Yes.  It would be great to get weekly update.

Andrew:  Actually, most of our customers prefer the email updates but we’ll definitely take it into consideration.  Have you ever won anything before?
Melissa :  No. I tried…but I never got anything.

Andrew:  Well, I’m happy that we changed that for you.  I hope that you enjoy the game!

After the interview, Melissa sent over a lovely photo of herself and her husband.  We were happy to send her the tickets to the game of her choice.  Then, she emailed us back,

“‪We got them!! Thank you so much! They are great seats!  :)‬”

Thank You, Melissa for Choosing OUR Energy!

OUR Energy would like to thank each and every one of the participants in our contest.  You too can be future NFL Sweepstakes Winners!We hope to have the opportunity to reach out to each of you in the future with an exciting “Congratulations!” phone call.

We promise to maintain our high standards of quality at the most affordable energy rates.
